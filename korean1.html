Korean 1

Korean 1 introduces students to Hangul, everyday greetings, and the foundations of Korean culture. Students build confidence with speaking, listening, reading, and writing through interactive activities.

← Back to catalog

What you will learn

  • Hangul reading and writing fluency
  • Basic vocabulary for school, family, and hobbies
  • Common greetings and polite expressions
  • Introduction to Korean geography and traditions

Highlights

  • Interactive speaking practice and partner work
  • Culture days featuring food, music, and games
  • Short dialogues and skits
  • Beginner-friendly projects

Recommended for

  • Students new to Korean language study
  • Anyone interested in K-culture and media
  • Learners who enjoy collaborative activities
