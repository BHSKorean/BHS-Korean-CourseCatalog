Korean 1 is an introductory course to the Korean language and the rich culture of Korea. This course is designed for students with little or no prior experience in Korean. Students will be introduced to fundamental structures and vocabulary relevant to everyday situations, empowering them to communicate effectively at a Novice Level in Korean. The course focuses on the development of the three modes of communication; Interpretive, Interpersonal, and Presentational as well as cultural awareness and an ability to make connections with students' own cultures. Both the Hangul writing system and basic Korean characters will be covered. Language functions and notions will be integrated into various topics and settings. Korean 1 offers a comprehensive, standards-based program of study aligned with the 3 C's of the California World Language Standards: Communication, Cultures, and Connections.
