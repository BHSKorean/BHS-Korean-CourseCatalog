Korean 2

Hello! 안녕하세요!

Korean 2 is a continuation for students who have completed Korean 1 or have equivalent prior experience in Korean. In this course, students will further develop their mastery of the Korean language skills, including speaking, listening, reading, and writing at a Novice High - Intermediate Low level. Emphasis will be placed on enhancing oral proficiency, expanding knowledge of Korean culture and history, and refining the skills necessary for effective communication. Students will gain confidence in using an extended vocabulary and practice conversational sentence patterns. Reading for content and basic writing skills will be introduced.
