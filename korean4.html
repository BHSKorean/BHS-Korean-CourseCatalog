Korean 4 / 4 Honors

Korean 4 and Korean 4 Honors are advanced courses designed for high levels of proficiency. Students work with authentic texts, refine academic writing, and engage in leadership roles within the program.

← Back to catalog

What you will learn

  • Advanced reading and interpretation of authentic texts
  • Academic writing and research skills
  • Nuanced discussion and debate strategies
  • Preparation for advanced language opportunities

Highlights

  • Leadership in cultural events and mentoring
  • Capstone-style projects and presentations
  • Analysis of literature, film, and current events
  • Optional honors extension activities

Recommended for

  • Students who completed Korean 3
  • Learners ready for honors-level challenges
  • Students planning to continue Korean in college
