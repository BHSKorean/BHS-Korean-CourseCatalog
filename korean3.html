Korean 3

You got this! 화이팅!

Korean 3 is designed to elevate students to a level where they can meet complex social demands in Korean society. This course provides an in-depth study of grammar, vocabulary, and Korean culture. Students will create sophisticated sentence structures for both formal and informal situations, expressing personal opinions on diverse topics at an Intermediate proficiency level. The course is conducted entirely in Korean, and the prerequisite is the completion of Korean 2 with a grade of "C" or better or a teacher's recommendation. By the end of the course, students should be able to comprehend significant ideas and details in authentic texts, understand oral and written discourse with few errors in comprehension, and demonstrate increasing fluency, control of vocabulary, and culturally appropriate behavior.
